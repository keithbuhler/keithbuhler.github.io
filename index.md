I'm a wisdom-seeker and Orthodox Christian educator and entrepreneur. 

I am currently an Honors College professor at Azusa Pacific University in CA, the founding headmaster and director of advancement at [St Andrew Academy](https://www.saintandrewacademy.com/), and asset manager at [Numinor Homes](https://www.numinorhomes.com/), an investment focused on thoughtful property acquisition and development. I'm also a west coast [Fellow at Alcuin.](https://alcuinfellowship.com/)

My life mission is to find wisdom and share it with others. 

That mission has lead me into various avenues: the classroom, fundraising, deal-making, business acquisition, and more. But my message is self-responsibility, the pursuit of virtue, and advocating for great education. None of this -- or anything else -- is possible without the help of Christ working in us. 

A life of prayer, wisdom, and joy is possible even in this crazy, chaotic, and fallen world. 

It starts with classical, Christian education. Christian homes with marriages intact. Parishes and schools that are humble and unified. People falling in love with beauty. Having fun with logical thinking. Fanatical and immoderate devotion to moderation.  Life-long learning. 

Living well is a skill. Happiness is a skill. It's an activity we can get better at. As adults, we lead the way for our children. 

Thanks for visiting, let me know how I can help you.
