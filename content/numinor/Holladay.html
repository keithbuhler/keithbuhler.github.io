Investment Thesis

25 Holladay Drive represents a unique transition opportunity. Following a rigorous $700,000 capital expenditure program over the last three years, the asset has moved from a "heavy-lift" value-add project to a stabilized property management play.

The heavy construction work—including a new roof on the north building (14 units), 10+ HVAC system upgrades, plumbing modernization, and unit conversions—- is complete. The current ownership has successfully proved the market, with top-of-market rents achieving $1,150/month. The acquisition allows a new operator to step in and normalize the rent roll with minimal capital requirement, capturing the remaining upside through operational excellence.

Management Opportunity

The current ownership is exiting due a successful value-add project managed from out of state. This asset is ideally suited for a local Kentucky operator or a regional firm with a strong property management infrastructure or an out of state investor with a strong local team.
