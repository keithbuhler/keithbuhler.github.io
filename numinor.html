Capital placement

$Small-to-medium

Your capital needs a home!

For small- to medium-sized sums, we can put your capital to work and return fixed, monthly payments at a generous return.

These funds fuel our current and future projects.

Monthly interest payments of .075%-1.5% of the capital allocated grow your investment with little risk, no long-term commitment, and no stress. That's an APR of 9%-18%, depending on the time, amount, and conditions of the capital placement.

Private capital placements are committed for 3-6 month but they are renewable: keep the returns going or cash out – it's up to you!

Who we are

Numinor is the passion project of Dr. Dionysius "Keith" Buhler.

Buhler is an entrepreneur and educator. He founded a non-profit school and a for-profit investment business in the same 12 month.

He began "house hacking" by living in a duplex in Lexington, KY during graduate school. He fell in love with Kentucky — the people, the beauty, and the dynamic real estate market.

In 2020 he founded Numinor to help others to achieve their investment goals by producing passive income.

What's in a name?

True Tolkien fans will recognize the elvish name of Westernesse, Numenor — the land of strong men who loved God and celebrated friendship with Elves.

Our name, Numinor, is based on the (mis)spelling in C. S. Lewis' Cosmic Trilogy, Book 3: [Merlin asks,] "Tell me… what is Numinor? The true West," said Ransom.

Numinor thus aims to represent the strength and blessing that land can be when we treat it as a gift to be cultivated, when men are true as the Dúnedain!
