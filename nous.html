The Context

Meeting the Moment

As converts pour into the Orthodox Church, the need for stable foundations has never been greater. We need more than just full pews; we need stable homes, a robust clergy, and a new generation of Orthodox teachers.

Currently, Orthodox parish schools, private schools, and homeschool co-ops remain challenging to launch and even harder to maintain. NOUS aims to bridge this gap, providing churches, communities, and families with the tools they need to thrive.

The 2035 Mandate:

35 thriving, sustainable Orthodox classical schools across the US by 2035.
